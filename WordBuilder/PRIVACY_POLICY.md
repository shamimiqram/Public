# Privacy Policy — Word Builder

**Last updated:** March 2026  
**App name:** Word Builder  
**Package name:** com.shamim.wordbuilder  
**Developer contact:** shamimeakram@gmail.com

---

## 1) Overview

Word Builder is an offline-first word puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains what information is handled when you use the app.

---

## 2) Data We Collect

### 2.1 We do not collect personal data on our servers
Word Builder does **not** require an account and we do **not** operate our own server to collect or store your personal information.

### 2.2 Data stored locally on your device (offline)
The app stores some information **only on your device** to make the game work:

- **Game progress:** levels completed, best scores, best times, daily streak, and saved/resume state
- **Settings:** language, selected mode, and other preferences
- **Profile (optional):** player name and profile photo (if you choose to set them)

This information stays in the app’s local storage and is not sent to us.

---

## 3) Third‑Party Services (Optional)

Depending on which features are enabled in your build and how you use the app, the app may interact with third‑party services:

- **Google Play Games (optional):** If you sign in, Google may provide your Play Games profile information (such as display name and avatar) to the app. Google may process this data according to their policies.
- **Advertising (optional, e.g., Google AdMob):** If ads are enabled, the ad provider may collect information such as device identifiers, ad interactions, and diagnostics to serve and measure ads.

These services are provided by Google and are governed by Google’s policies:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy  
- Google Ads technologies: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

If you want a strictly offline experience, you can use the app without signing in and (if applicable) without viewing ads.

---

## 4) How We Use Data

We use locally stored data only to:
- Save your progress and preferences
- Display your optional profile name/photo inside the app
- Enable optional features you choose to use (e.g., Play Games sign‑in, ads)

---

## 5) Data Sharing

We do not sell your data.

We do not share personal data with third parties except as needed for optional third‑party features (for example, Google Play Games or ads, if enabled).

---

## 6) Data Retention

- **On-device data** remains until you clear the app storage or uninstall the app.
- **Third‑party data** (if any) is handled and retained according to the third party’s policies (e.g., Google).

---

## 7) Children’s Privacy

Word Builder is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children on our servers. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us at **shamimeakram@gmail.com**.

---

## 8) Your Choices and Controls

- You can change or remove your **player name** and **profile photo** from the Profile screen.
- You can delete all locally stored data by clearing the app’s data from Android Settings or uninstalling the app.

---

## 9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will be changed when we update it.
